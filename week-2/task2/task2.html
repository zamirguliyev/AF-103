Homemade Apple Pie Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 6-8 medium-sized apples(peeled,cored,and slice)
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 2 tablespoons butter(cut into small pieces)
  • 2 unbaked pie crusts(homemade or store-bought)

Instructions:

  1. preheat your oven to 425 F(220C)
  2. In a large mixing bowl combune the sliced sugar floiur cinnamin and salt.Toos until the apples are evenly coated
  3. Place one of the pie crusts into a 9-inch pie dish
  4. Pour the apple mixture into the pie crust spreading it out evenly
  5. Dot the top of the apple mixture with small pieces of butter
  6. Place the second pie crust oven the apples and crimp the edges to seal the piw
  7. Using a sharp knife make a few small slits in the top crust to allow steam to escape
  8. Bake the pie in the preheated ovem for 45-55 minutues or untill the crustin golden brown and filling is bubbling
  9. Remove the pie from the oven allow it ti cool for least 30 minutues before serving

Serving Suggestion:

Sserve your homemade apple pie warm with a scoop of vanilla ice cream for a delicious tret

Enjoy!
